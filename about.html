Squid Mountain is the last band left on Earth. We have pledged a sacred bond to preserve the music of Old Earth.


Matt Malott (AKA Pepsi Maxx): Vox + Guitar.
Pepsi Maxx was born on a farm out by New Windsor. He spent his nuclear winters in Detroit.

Kirsten Kirsch (AKA Curious George): Vox.
Curious George is the last surviving descendant of Cher. She is classically trained.

Peter Angelinos (AKA Lucky Strike): Guitar.
Lucky Strike was found by the band in a burned-out Costco. He speaks no English.

Subhayu Mukherjee (AKA Jethro McGillicuddy): Bass.
Jethro McGillicuddy hails from a Nuevo Scotia fishing village. He owns the last surviving Giorgio Armani.

Sandy Zelazny (AKA Marvin the Martian): Drums.
Marvin the Martian built her first kit from reeds and hula hoops. She got rhythm.
